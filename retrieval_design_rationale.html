Inference Time Pipeline Retrieval Design Rationale
Three design decisions behind the knowledge-graph retrieval module
01
Why Two Retrieval Strategies
  • Depth — single-layer vs. two-layer: does deeper inference add recall?
  • Filtering — fixed Top-K vs. adaptive weight: how does candidate quality change?
02
Why K = 5 (Strategy 1)
  • Tuned empirically
  • Larger K → more low-relevance candidates → more false "Yes" in VQA → more hallucinations
  • K=5 sharply improves candidate precision
03
Why Mean + Std (Strategy 2)
  • Mean alone lets too many low-relevance candidates through
  • +1 std narrows the pool to the strongly associated nodes
  • Improves downstream VQA quality
