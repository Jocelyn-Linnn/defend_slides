Divergent Trends Across the Three Metrics
Why a drop in RadGraph / GREEN doesn't mean the system failed
01
Fact-Consistency vs. Coverage
↓
RadGraph F1
↑
CheXBert Micro F1
RadGraph penalizes synonym mismatches, not wrong findings
Example
GT"mild pulmonary vascular congestion (肺血管充血)"
MedGemmaMissed it entirely
SystemCorrected with "subtle pulmonary edema (肺水腫)"
CheXBertRecognizes both as Edema（水腫） → Micro F1 +0.190
RadGraph"edema"（水腫） ≠ "vascular congestion"（血管充血） as strings → no TP, counted as FP → F1 drops
02
Semantic Quality vs. Coverage
↓
GREEN
↑
CheXBert Micro F1
CheXBert checks disease presence; GREEN checks whole-report fidelity
Example
GT"retrocardiac opacity（心臟後方陰影）"
MedGemmaMissed it (not mentioned)
SystemAdded it correctly, but also hallucinated "linear opacity（線狀陰影） suggestive of a scar（疤痕）"
CheXBertCounts it as Lung Opacity（肺部陰影） TP → Micro F1 → 1.000
GREENPenalizes the hallucinated scar（疤痕） → GREEN −0.102
