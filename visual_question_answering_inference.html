Visual Question Answering Inference
Re-grounding retrieved questions in the image itself, not just the free-text report
01
Visual Question Answering
MedGemma Re-Answers Each Retrieved Question
Reloads MedGemma (medgemma-1.5-4b-it) and asks every knowledge-graph-retrieved question directly against the image — grounding each candidate finding in visual evidence rather than free-text generation alone.
Example
Q"Do you see any sign of pneumonia in the image?"
ANo. There are no clear signs of pneumonia in the image. The lung fields appear relatively clear, with no consolidation or infiltrates visible.
Forced format — every answer starts with Yes/No, followed by a 1–2 sentence justification, for consistent and parseable output
02
Structured Merge
