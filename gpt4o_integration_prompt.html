GPT-4o Integration Prompt
How five prompt rules turn initial report + confirmed QA into the final report
01
5 Integration Rules
What the GPT-4o System Prompt Enforces
1
Initial report has highest priority — QA may only supplement, never overwrite (QA-first ordering was tested and hurt accuracy)
2
Image-quality exemptions — severely damaged or completely normal images skip QA integration entirely
3
4-way QA classification — CONFIRMED kept; MIXED trimmed to its confirmed part; DIFFERENTIAL & NORMAL discarded
4
Negative statements only from the initial report — QA can never introduce a new "no X" finding
5
Output format — 3–10 sentence paragraph, no bullets/bold/brackets, preserve original wording (temperature = 0)
02
Worked Example
Supplementing, Not Overwriting
Example
MedGemma (Initial) Status post sternotomy with tubes in place; moderate left pleural effusion with atelectasis; patchy opacity in the right lower lung, c/w pneumonia; heart size normal; no pneumothorax
QA (Yes only, filtered) "Subsegmental atelectasis in the lower lung fields, left side" · "Evidence of parapneumonic effusion"
Final Report (GPT-4o) ...moderate left effusion with atelectasis. Subsegmental atelectasis noted in the lower lung fields, left side. Patchy opacity in the right lower lung, c/w pneumonia. Evidence of parapneumonic effusion is present. Heart size normal, no pneumothorax.
