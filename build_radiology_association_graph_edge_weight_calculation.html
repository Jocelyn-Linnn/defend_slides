Build Radiology Association Graph - Edge Weight Calculation
1
Statistics
Scan all SUGGESTIVE_OF relations and accumulate 4 metrics:
edge Occurrences of "A→B" (Numerator)
source Total relations out from Source
target Total relations into Target
total Global sum of all SUGGESTIVE_OF edges
2
Method 1 — Conditional Probability
Probability of pointing to B given out-edges from A.
Sum of out-edge weights from the same source ≤ 1.
weight = count(A→B) / count(A→*)
3
Method 2 — NPMI
Penalizes highly frequent targets to highlight relationships with clinical specificity. Range: [−1, 1]
NPMI = PMI / −log P(A→B)
Why both — CP overestimates frequent targets; NPMI corrects via marginal probabilities to surface clinically specific pairs. Comparing both tests which definition retrieves better.
