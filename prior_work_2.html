Report Quality Challenges
Missed Findings（遺漏發現）
Subtle lesions（病灶）and multi-lesion co-occurrence frequently undetected
Hallucinations（幻覺／捏造發現）
False findings（虛假發現） — non-existent pathology（病理表徵）reported as present
Polarity inversion（極性反轉） — present pathology stated as absent
Comparison bias（比較偏誤） — fabricated "no change vs. prior" from MIMIC training distribution
Measurement errors（測量誤差） — device positions, organ sizes, lesion（病灶）dimensions
Root issue: VLMs cannot self-correct.
