Graph Design Goals
The graph's job: surface clinical associations the initial report may have missed
1
Compact — No Duplicate Nodes
The same clinical concept, seen across thousands of reports, collapses into a single node instead of one per mention
2
Observation-Based
Each node stores what was observed (present / absent / uncertain), where (anatomical location), and how severe (modifier)
opacity → OBS-DP-E  (finding)
mild     → OBS-U-M  (severity)
lung      → ANAT-E  (location)
3
Nodes Carry Inferential Relations
A SUGGESTIVE_OF edge connects one finding to another it clinically implies — letting the graph surface findings the report never stated
low lung volume ⟶ bibasilar atelectasis ⟶ early pneumonia
