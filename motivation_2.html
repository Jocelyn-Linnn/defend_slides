Motivation
Clinical Challenge High Radiologist Workload CXR（胸部X光）is the most frequent imaging exam. Radiologists under high workload need automated report generation support.
VLM Limitation Hallucination & Missing Findings VLM-based report generation achieves fluency but suffers from hallucinations, missed lesions（病灶）, and clinical inaccuracy.
Existing Approaches RAG & Graph RAG Focus on Generation Methods like RAG and Graph RAG improve report generation, but most still focus on the generation stage rather than correcting already-generated reports.
Research Gap No Systematic Post-Generation Refinement A structured mechanism to correct errors, hallucinations, and omissions in already-generated reports is still missing.
Research Direction
Based on these research gaps and motivations, this study adopts graph-based post-generation correction as its research direction
